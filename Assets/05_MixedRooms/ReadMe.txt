INDOOR SPORTS SERIES
====================

This is a fantastic triple pack of indoor sports venue rooms - a retro pool room, a converted warehouse and a city bar environment.

Mix and match 3 unique room prefabs with over 70 different furniture prefabs, including arcade cab and jukeboxes, bars, sofas, chairs, tables, pictures, lights, lamps, drum kit, neon signs, air con units, speakers, kitchen area and a game ready Pool/Billiards table with a full set of Pool balls and cues.

You can also mix materials to redecorate the rooms. Limitless options are available.

All furniture and rooms are Prefabs. Lighting is set to real-time in the demo scenes, but can easily be baked.

Textures are not atlased to ensure a good and consistent Texel resolution, many include normal maps.

The package comes with three example demo scenes.
